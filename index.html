Vision

Creativity without constraints.

At Orbit, we believe creativity should never be limited by the tools you use. For too long, 3D design has been confined to bulky desktops, complicated interfaces, and rigid workflows that slow down the creative process. We started Orbit to change that. Our vision is to create a more natural, portable, and comfortable way to interact with 3D design.

We are building an ecosystem of high-quality tools that bring freedom and intuition back to the creative process. With Orbitpad, designers can model entire projects through simple, customizable hotkeys made for sketching, extruding, and building ideas without ever needing a full keyboard setup. With Orbitmouse, we are unlocking true six degrees of freedom control, allowing you to rotate, tilt, push, and pull your models just like you would in the real world. Unlike other 3D mice that require proprietary software or constant driver installations, Orbitmouse works instantly as a Bluetooth peripheral. It connects seamlessly to any device, so you can design wherever inspiration strikes without setup barriers or technical friction.

Our goal is to make 3D design accessible anywhere. Whether you are working from a laptop, an iPad, or a headset like Apple Vision Pro, Orbit gives you the same power and precision that used to demand a full studio setup. We want creativity to feel effortless, where every movement and gesture directly shapes your ideas, bridging the gap between imagination and execution.

Orbit is more than a hardware company. It is a movement toward the future of 3D design—a future built on freedom, comfort, and adaptability. We are designing tools that disappear into the background, letting your ideas take center stage.

Orbitmouse

Previewing Orbitmouse…

Orbitmouse, designed for flow.

Orbitmouse is a compact, weighted controller that gives you instant 3D navigation without breaking your rhythm. Its low-profile form fits comfortably under your palm, letting you orbit, pan, and zoom with subtle, intuitive movements.

Connect Orbitmouse with the app to fine-tune sensitivity, assign gestures to CAD commands, and sync settings across every workspace you use. Keep your workflow calibrated whether you are at the studio, on site, or designing in transit.

Command in reach

Orbitpad compresses hundreds of shortcuts into an intuitive layout. Assign macros, cycle tools, and navigate views with minimal movement.

Built for endurance

CNC-machined aluminum chassis with tactile optical switches make Orbitpad a durable companion for long CAD sessions.

Context-aware lighting

Orbitpad's adaptive backlighting guides your workflow. Match color profiles to popular CAD suites or your custom profiles.

Orbitpad Tech Specs

Materials

6000-series aluminum, PBT keycaps, silicone base

Switches

Optical low-travel switches rated for 70M actuations

Connectivity

USB-C, Bluetooth 5.3 with multi-device pairing

Software

Orbit Studio for macOS & Windows; API for custom integrations

Dimensions

143 × 96 × 24 mm · 420 g

Battery

Li-ion 2800 mAh · Up to 40 hours with adaptive lighting
